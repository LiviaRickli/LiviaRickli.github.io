ABOUT ME

WHO I AM

I am a UX & UI designer, currently living in Amsterdam, with a passion for creating online & offline designs that are useful, distinctive and meaningful to users.

Away from designing & building things, I love watching movies based on true stories, ski holidays and hanging out with my family & friends.

WHAT I DO

I currently work for Sogeti, a leading company which provides technology solutions, based in 15 countries and has a strong local presence in over 100 locations in Europe, USA and India.

I’m always up for new challenges, so get in touch if you think we’d make wonderful things together.

BACKGROUND

I was born in Switzerland (Lachen) and moved to the Netherlands when I was 3 years old. I have a twin brother and a older brother, which I love very much. For me family and friends are always number one.

When I was 12 I became a professional athlete (basketball), for which I trained nearly every day. Therefore I moved a lot, but because of that I got to know a lot of people and places. Eventually I ended up in Amsterdam, where I’m enjoying myself immensely.

EXPERIENCE

As a former professional athlete (basketball) I have a lot of experience working in teams and taking my responsibilities within a team. In addition, I also worked as a Speech therapist, for which I completed a bachelor. In this role I developed good presentation and communication skills.

In 2012 I started my second bachelor Communication and Multimedia Design, which I hope to finish soon. Combining a full time job and bachelor study was very tough, but I’m very happy that I made a career switch. I think its very important to do what makes you happy.

I’m always astonished by a forest. It makes me realise that the fantasy of nature is much larger than my own fantasy. I still have things to learn.
