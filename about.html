WHAT I DO

I currently work for Raet (seconded from Sogeti) as UX Designer. I create interaction models and designs for digital channels and new digital concepts.

I’m always up for new challenges, so get in touch if you think we’d make wonderful things together.

BACKGROUND

I was born in Switzerland (Lachen) and moved to the Netherlands when I was 3 years old. I have a twin brother and a older brother, whom I love very much. For me family and friends are always number one.

When I was 12 I became a professional athlete (basketball), for this I trained nearly every day. Therefore I moved a lot, but because of that I got to know a lot of people and places. Eventually I ended up in Amsterdam, where I’m enjoying myself immensely.

EXPERIENCE

As a former professional athlete (basketball) I have a lot of experience working in teams and taking my responsibilities within a team. In addition, I also worked as a Speech therapist, for which I completed a bachelor's degree. In this role I developed good presentation and communication skills.

In 2012 I started my second bachelor Communication and Multimedia Design. I’m very happy that I made a career switch, because I think it's very important to do what makes you happy.
